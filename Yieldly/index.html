Detectable Diseases

5 critical eggplant diseases,
instantly identified.

Our machine learning model is trained on the most common and devastating diseases affecting Philippine eggplant farmers.

🦠
Bacterial Wilt
Ralstonia solanacearum
Sudden wilting of shoots and leaves, often irreversible without early intervention.
🟤
Phomopsis Blight
Phomopsis vexans
Fruit rot and stem lesions that spread rapidly under humid conditions.
🍃
Cercospora Leaf Spot
Cercospora melongenae
Circular brown lesions on leaves that reduce photosynthesis and crop yield.
🐛
Fruit & Shoot Borer
Leucinodes orbonalis
Bored holes in shoots and fruits, causing significant post-harvest losses.
✅
Healthy Plant
No disease detected
Your crop looks healthy! Continue regular monitoring and preventive care.
How It Works

Simple as taking a photo.

Designed for farmers with no technical background. No account, no downloads, no complicated steps.

01
📱
Open the app
Open Yieldy in your phone's browser — no app store download required. Works on any Android.
02
📸
Photograph your crop
Tap "Check My Crop" and take a clear photo of the affected leaf, fruit, or stem.
03
⚡
AI analyzes it
Our EfficientNet model classifies the image against 5 disease classes in under 3 seconds.
04
💊
Get your treatment plan
Receive the disease name, confidence score, and step-by-step treatment — in Filipino or Bisaya.
Technology

Built for rural reality.

Every technology choice prioritizes low-cost devices, offline capability, and Filipino farmers first.

Frontend
React.js PWA
Offline-capable
Installable on low-cost Android without app store. Works even with poor signal.
Backend
Python FastAPI
Lightweight
Single /predict endpoint. Minimal overhead for fast response times on rural networks.
ML Model
EfficientNet-B0
Transfer learning
Smaller and faster than custom CNN. High accuracy even with limited local training data.
Hosting
Railway / Render
Free tier
No infrastructure cost during development and testing. Scales easily for production.
